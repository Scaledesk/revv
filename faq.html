Getting Started

What is Switch ?

Switch is a first of its kind car subscription program in India. You can subscribe by month or year, with options like turning it on/off anytime, swapping between car models and swicthing across cities.

Which cities is Switch available in?

Switch is currently available in Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, DelhiNCR, Mumbai and Pune.

Am I eligible to use Switch?

As long as you are above 21 years of age, and have a valid driving license (for Light Motor Vehicles) that is at least 1 year old, you are welcome to use Switch.

What documentation will be required?

We will need to see one original ID proof (either PAN Card, or Passport, or Aadhar Card), plus your original driving license. Driving license printed on A4 sheet of paper (original or otherwise) will not be considered as a valid document.

What do I need to pay to get started?

For “by the month” option, you need to pay 1 month’s subscription fee in advance, plus a refundable security deposit equivalent to 1 month’s subscription fee.
For “by the year” option, you need to pay 1 month’s subscription fee (=annual fee, divided by 12) and 1 month’s maintenance fee in advance, plus a refundable security deposit equivalent to 2 months’ subscription fee. In addition you will need to pay for the annual insurance of the car.

Can I share my car with someone and split costs ?

Yes, of course! You only need to let us know that you are sharing it, and send us a scanned copy of the driving license of your sharing partner/s.

Where do I need to pickup and return the car?

Switch is available at your doorstep. We will deliver the car and also pick it up.

What will be the quality of the car delivered to me?

The car would be as good as new. All cars in our fleet are very well maintained, and have low kilometers (<30K) on the odometer. As an inaugural offer, we will provide brand-new cars for the first 30 bookings on “by the year” plan.

Switching on and off

Can I switch off whenever I want?

Yes. You just need to inform us, and your subscription will automatically conclude at the end of the month, with no further billing next month onwards.

How do I switch off ?

Just place a “switch off” request on the Switch app (coming soon), at least 7 days prior to your switch off time. Or just give a call to your dedicated concierge. We will have the car picked up from your doorstep, at your preferred date and time.

Is there any penalty associated with switching off ?

No. Switch payments work on a monthly basis, and once you switch off, we will stop billing you from next month onwards. Your subscription for the last paid month will continue to be active till the end of that month.

How do I switch on again after switching off ?

Simple. Just place a “switch on again” request on the Switch app (coming soon), and pick your start date, plan and car model. Your car will be delivered to your doorstep.

Switching between cars and cities

Can I switch between cars?

Yes you can, subject to availablility.

What kind of cars can I switch between ?

Between any car models in our fleet. Including those that are not included in the plan selected by you. This is how it works:
- If you switch to a car model included in the plan that you are subscribed to, your monthly fee stays the same
If you switch to a car model from a different plan, your monthly fee would change. The monthly fee of the new plan (that is, the plan to which the new model selected by you belongs) will apply. For example, if you switch from an SUV to a hatchback, your monthly fee will reduce
Switching would be subject to availability of the car model selected, and in rare cases, we might need to request you to wait for a few days till your preferred model becomes available.

Which cities can I switch across ?

To any city where Switch services are offered (currently Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, DelhiNCR (Mumbai, Pune?)) Switching across cities will be subject to the availability of your preferred model in the destination city.

How do I switch between cars and cities ?

By placing a request on the switch app (coming soon), or by calling your dedicated concierge. Please note that placing a request at least 24 hours prior would be ideal, because it will allow us to identify the best car available for you, and get it in top-shape (e.g., dry cleaning) before delivering it to you.

How frequently can I switch ?

If you subscribe to Switch by the month, you would be entitled to 1 Switch credit for every month of your subscription. You can accumulate your Switch credits, and use them whenever you want. For example, if you have accumulated 6 Switch credits, you can even Switch daily for 6 continuous days.
Switch credits are not included in “by the year” subscription, but can be purchased separately.

Is switching free ?

If you subscribe “by the month”, you only need to pay Rs 1000 per switch towards processing, cleaning and delivery charges. If you subscribe “by the year”, you can buy Switch credits @ Rs 3000 per credit, including processing, cleaning and delivery charges

What are switch credits ?

Switch credits are like coins that you need to use for switching across car models or switching across cities. For every completed month of your subscription, you earn 1 Switch credit.
When you switch from car model A to car model B, you can do so by using 1 Switch credit. Similarly when you switch from city A to city B, you can do so by using 1 Switch credit. When you switch again (including switching back to the original car model or city), you will need to use another Switch credit.
You can accumulate credits during your subscription period, and can use them whenever you want to (during the subscription period). Switch credits expire when you switch off the subscription.

Can I buy additional switch credits ?

Yes, you can buy Switch credits @ Rs 3000 per credit, including processing, cleaning and delivery charges.

Can I accumulate switch credits ?

You can accumulate credits during your subscription period, and can use them whenever you want to (during the subscription period). Switch credits expire when you switch off the subscription.

Can I transfer switch credits to a friend ?

No, Switch credits are not transferable.

Pricing

What is included in the prices ?

If you subscribe “by the month”, pricing includes subscription fee, insurance and maintenance. Prices exclude fuel - we will deliver the car with a full tank, and will need you to return it with a full tank at the end of your subscription period.
If you subscribe “by the year”, pricing includes subscription fee. You will need to buy yearly insurance separately (just like you do for your own car), and you will need to pay additionally for maintenance at a pre-defined rate for every kilometre driven. Prices exclude fuel.

What is excluded from the prices ?

“By the month” subscription prices exclude fuel, tolls, parking, inter-state taxes. “By the year” subscription prices exclude insurance, maintenance, fuel, tolls, parking, inter-state taxes.

Who pays for fuel, tolls, parking, inter-state taxes ?

You will pay for these (just like you do in your own car).

What is maintenance fee ?

In “by the year” option, you need to pay a separate maintenance fee separately, based on your actual usage. It will be billed on “Rs per km” basis, and will cover all scheduled services and normal wear-and-tear (e.g., replacement of tyres, batteries, air filters, fluids etc). It will not cover unusual wear-and-tear (e.g., a clutch burn-out caused by clutch over-riding)

How will payment work ?

For both “by the month” and “by the year” plans, your monthly fee will be billed at the start of every month, through an auto-debit setup on your credit/debit card, or through an electronic clearing arrangement with your bank.

Is there a “Kms limit” to how much I can drive ?

Yes, there is a monthly limit of 2500 Kms (=30,000 Kms per year). This limit has been designed to ensure that it should suffice for the vast majority of private usage requirements. You can still drive beyond the kilometres limit, but the additional kilometres clocked will attract an additional charge.

Are there any restrictions to where I can travel ?

All of our cars are equipped with an All India Tourist Permit, so you are free to drive anywhere in the country.
All India Tourist Permit means that the car is legally permitted to ply in any state in the country, after paying the inter-state taxes at the state borders. However, we do not permit taking the cars to Leh/Ladakh region, because of local unrest there against external cars.
We also advise you to avoid bad terrains (especially in non-SUV cars) and areas affected by civil unrest.

What happens if I return the car AFTER the end of my subscription period ?

We request you NOT to do this. Once we receive a “switch off” request from you, we will assign the car to another user. A delay in returning the car can severely impair our ability to deliver the car on-time to the next user. Discourage this, we will be compelled to charge you twice (2x) the pro-rata fee for the delay period.

What if I switch off a “by the year” plan mid-way through the year ?

n such a situation, your subscription will get considered as a “by the month” subscription, and your total bill will be re-estimated by multiplying your months of usage with the monthly fee. From this total bill, we will subtract the the insurance and maintenance cost already paid by you, and we will deduct the balance amount from your security deposit.
For example:
If your annual subscription fee is Rs. 2,40,000 (incl. taxes), and you return the car after 7 months of usage, you would have already paid Rs 1,40,000 (=7 months * 240000/12) in the monthly subscription fee payments In addition, you would have paid Rs 35,000 in monthly maintenance fee (=Rs 2 per km * 2500 Kms per month * 7 months),
and annual insurance cost of Rs 55,000. Thus, the total payment made by you till the end of 7 months would be Rs 2,30,000 (= 1,40,000 + 35,000 + 55,000) Now, if the monthly fee for the “by the month” option for the same plan is Rs 35,000 (incl. taxes), your total bill for 7 months would be re-estimated to Rs 2,45,000 (=35,000 * 7)
The difference between the above 2 is Rs 15,000, which will be deducted from your security deposit, and the balance would be refunded to you within 7 days of ending your subscription.

Insurance and maintenance

What if I am involved in an accident? What is my liability ?

In the event of damages, your liability is limited to a maximum of Rs 10000. You will need to pay Damage Fee equivalent to the repair cost (as estimated by an authorized dealership) and downtime losses, upto a maximum of Rs 10000. This is independent of the nature and terms of the insurance cover that we have on the cars.
Please note that the maximum liability policy is intended to benefit and protect the responsible user. In situations of irresponsible or unsafe or negligent usage, there could be discretionary exceptions to this policy, whereby the user could be liable for all damages irrespective of whether or not we are able to claim insurance in such cases. Examples of such situation include, but are not limited to:

  • When the User is not in compliance with the laws and/or with terms of the Switch User Agreement
  • When the User is found to be under the influence of alcohol/narcotics while driving
  • When the User is over-speeding (i.e., above 125 Kms/Hr)
  • When the User is in violation of traffic rules or of the Motor Vehicles Act (for example, if the User has not paid the inter-state tax while crossing a state border)
  • When the person driving the vehicle at the time of the incident is found to be different than the users’ details shared with us
  • When the vehicle workshop and/or insurance company deem the damage to be consequential in nature (this will be clearly stated in the accident report).
  • Consequential damage occurs when the User continues driving the vehicle even when it is performing abnormally
  • When the number of people in the car at the time of the incident exceed the standard seating capacity of the car
  • When the incident happened in a state for which the user had failed to pay the inter-state tax while entering the state
  • When there is misrepresentation of information in the driving license and ID proof details shared by the user
  • Where there is clear evidence of extremely rash and negligent driving on the user’s part
  • In cases where the estimated damage charges exceed the security deposit, we will request the user to pay the same at the time of returning the car. In cases of non-payment, we reserve the right of reporting it to credit bureau and reporting to other authorities or persons.
  • We avoid charging for minor damages (e.g., minor scratches and dents), and cover the vast majority of damage incidents under the maximum liability policy.
  • In cases of collision or User failure, towing and impounding charges will be paid by the User. In cases of vehicle failure, towing and impounding charges will be paid by us.

What do I do if the vehicle breaks down or if I meet with an accident ?

We will replace your car with a different one, within a week of the incident. In such cases, please notify us immediately (by calling your dedicate concierge) and we will guide you through the entire process. We will coordinate with the road-side assistance and insurance. Unless unavoidable, please do not leave the scene of the accident until proper support has arrived. You may be required to file/sign formality documents associated with the accident.

What if the car gets stolen ?

The maximum liability policy described earlier in this section will apply to theft cases also.

Who takes care of regular servicing of the car ?

We will take care of it. For all scheduled services, we will notify you 2 days in advance. We will have the car picked up from your place, get it serviced, and deliver it back to you. We will also have a flexibility of few days on the service schedule, to accomodate any specific requirements that you might have (say, if you cannot give the car for servicing for another week, that would be ok in most cases).

Who takes care of wear-and-tear or hitches in the car’s performance ?

If you face any issues with the car performance, please inform us. We will have the car picked up from your place, get it repaired, and deliver it back to you.

Does it include road-side assistance ?

Yes, we provide road-side assistance with all the cars available on the Switch platform.

Others

What is dedicated concierge ?

Dedicated concierge means a dedicated contact person/number for each Switch subscriber, trained and prepared to handle all your requests quickly and smoothly.

What kind of registration and number plates will my car have ?

Cars will carry a black number plate, which belongs to an elite category of commercially registered cars, which can be driven by the private driving license holder.

Can I use the car for ANYTHING ?

Yes, except for commercial purposes.

What are the requirements if I drive across a state border ?

Each State has different procedures and regulations. Most States will require the car to stop at the border RTO station to pay the relevant tolls/taxes.
All Switch cars are equipped with original copies of the RC Card, Insurance Note, Self-Drive License, and the All India Permit, which will help you get through this conveniently. For many states, the state entry tax can be paid online as well, which your dedicated concierge can help you with.
