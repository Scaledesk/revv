About Switch

What does a “plan” mean ?

A plan means a curated set of car models. Each plan comes with a pre-defined monthly subscription fee, and once you subscribe to that plan, you can pick any car model from that set, and can switch across car models in that plan.

Can I pick any car within a plan ?

Yes. You can also switch across car models included in that plan.

Which cities is Switch available in ?

Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, DelhiNCR (Mumbai, Pune?)

What documentation will be required ?

We will need to see one original ID proof (either PAN Card, or Passport, or Aadhar Card), plus your original driving license. Driving license printed on A4 sheet of paper (original or otherwise) will not be considered as a valid document.

What do I need to pay to get started ?

For “by the month” option, you need to pay 1 month’s subscription fee in advance, plus a refundable security deposit equivalent to 1 month’s subscription fee.
For “by the year” option, you need to pay 1 month’s subscription fee (=annual fee, divided by 12) and 1 month’s maintenance fee in advance, plus a refundable security deposit equivalent to 2 months’ subscription fee. In addition you will need to pay for the annual insurance of the car.

Can I share my car with someone and split costs ?

Yes, of course! You only need to let us know that you are sharing it, and sned us a scan of the driving license of your sharing partner.

What will be the quality of the car delivered to me ?

The car would be as good as new. All cars in our fleet are very well maintained, and have low kilometers (< 30K) on the odo. As an inaugural offer, we will provide brand-new cars for the first 30 bookings on “by the year” plan.
